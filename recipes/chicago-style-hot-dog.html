Chicago-Style Hot Dog

Description

The Chicago-Style Hot Dog is like a regular hot dog, but it sets itself apart with the additions of vegetables such as onions and pickles, along with condiments like yellow mustard and dark green relish.

Ingredients

  • 1 beef hot dog
  • 1 poppy seed hot dog bun
  • tablespoon yellow mustard
  • tablespoon sweet green pickle relish
  • tablespoon chopped onion
  • 4 tomato wedges
  • 1 dill pickle spear
  • 2 sport peppers
  • 1 dash celery salt

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water over high heat.
  2. Reduce heat to low, place hot dog in water.
  3. Cook until warmed through, or about 5 minutes.
  4. Remove hot dog from water and set aside.
  5. Place a steamer basket into the pot.
  6. Steam the poppy seed bun until warm, about 2 minutes.
  7. Place hot dog in steamed bun.
  8. Put on toppings in this order: mustard, relish, onion, tomato, pickle, peppers, and celery salt.
  9. Nestle the tomato wedges between the hot dog and top of bun on one side.
  10. Place pickle between hot dog and bottom of bun on the other side.
